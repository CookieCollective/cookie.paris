Cookie Collective

The Collective gathers digital artists involved in real-time creation. This covers video games, art installations, video mapping, demoscene, live coding, etc.

We organize the Cookie Demoparty, a digital art festival which brings together various communities and features demos, experimental video games, machinimas, and concerts.

We also regularly prepare live coding parties, in which artists train and create visuals and sounds in front of the audience.

Lastly, the collective publishes a fanzine, which puts the spotlights on various aspects of the digital creation.

Go to the Fanzine page where you can read them online!

If you wish to support the Cookie Collective in producing fanzines and organizing events, please donate via PayPal with the message "Cookie Collective".
